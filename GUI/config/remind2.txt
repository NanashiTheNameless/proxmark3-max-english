Firmware flashing instructions:
1. First, click the bootrom button. Once the port is recognized normally, automatic flashing will begin. After successful flashing, the prompt "HAVE A NICE DAY" will appear. Press Enter to close the window.
2. After successfully flashing BOOT, click the fullimage button.

If flashing fails on the first attempt, try again — failures can sometimes be caused by the port number changing between attempts.
If you are on Windows 7 and the driver is lost during flashing, reinstall the driver. Drivers can be found in the other tools folder.
If no port is shown after connecting PM3 to the computer, unplug the cable, hold the side button on the PM3 (do not release), then plug the USB cable back in while continuing to hold the button. Wait for the port to appear, then flash the firmware. Flash BOOT first, then FULLIMAGE, then release the button. Do not release the button at any point during this process.
This firmware is designed for PM3-MAX. Do not flash this firmware on other devices — doing so may cause damage.

After flashing the firmware, unplug the device and reinsert it!
