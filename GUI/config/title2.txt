This software is for RFID technology research only and must not be used for illegal operations.
This software is free to use. You may submit bug reports or suggestions on the GitHub project page.
This software is designed for PM3 MAX hardware. Do not flash firmware on other devices without authorization.
Left-click "Auto Crack" to crack standard cards. Right-click "Auto Crack" to crack third-generation cards with no known vulnerabilities. After cracking succeeds, left-click "Auto Crack" to read the card. If cracking fails, try repositioning the card — for example, placing it on the back of the device.
Right-click "Force Read" to read card data first. Left-click the corresponding sector in the password area, then right-click to crack a single sector. This operation can bypass fully encrypted third-generation cards.
